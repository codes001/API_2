Syntax

PARAMETERS
--> type: (required)
Possible Value: market | limit | stop | close | cancel-limit | cancel-stop | cancel-all | tp-sl
--> side:
Possible Value: buy | sell
--> Other parameters :
symbol (string), price (number), quantity (number), tp (number), sl (number)

Examples

Market Order

type='market', symbol='PERP_BTC_USDT', side='buy', quantity=1000

Limit Order

type='limit', symbol='PERP_BTC_USDT', side='buy', price=2500, quantity=1000

Stop-Market Order

type='stop', symbol='PERP_BTC_USDT', side='buy', price=2500, quantity=1000

Close Position

type='close', symbol='PERP_BTC_USDT', side='buy', quantity=1000

Note: Make sure to specify that 'type' as 'close'. \n The 'side' is the direction of the original position you want to close

Cancel-Limit

type='cancel-limit', symbol='PERP_BTC_USDT'

Cancel-Stop

type='cancel-stop', symbol='PERP_BTC_USDT, side='buy'

Cancel-All

type='cancel-all', symbol='PERP_BTC_USDT, side='buy'

Take Profit and Stop Loss

type='tp-sl', symbol='PERP_BTC_USDT, side='buy', tp=5000, sl=1000
